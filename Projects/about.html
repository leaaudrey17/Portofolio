About Picverse

Learn more about our digital art community platform

Our Story

In 2015, Lea Audrey—then a digital artist—struggled to find a community that truly understood the challenges of digital creation. This inspired her to envision a platform where artists could connect, grow, and share their work.

By 2018, she gathered a small team to build what would become Picverse. Starting as a simple portfolio site, it quickly evolved into a global creative hub.

Now as CEO, Lea leads Picverse with a clear mission: to empower digital artists through connection, inspiration, and community.

Our Vision

To create a world where digital artists are recognized, celebrated, and empowered. We envision a global community where creativity knows no bounds, and artists from all backgrounds can connect, collaborate, and thrive together in a supportive environment that nurtures talent and innovation. We see Picverse as the catalyst for a new era of digital art appreciation, where creators receive the recognition they deserve and where art enthusiasts can discover and engage with extraordinary talent from around the world.

Our Mission

To build and nurture an inclusive platform that allows digital artists to share their work, interact with the community, and get recognized for their talent. We're committed to providing tools and opportunities that help artists grow, learn, and succeed in their creative journey while fostering meaningful connections between creators and art enthusiasts. Through continuous innovation and community engagement, we strive to break down barriers in the digital art world, making it more accessible, supportive, and rewarding for everyone involved. Our platform is designed to amplify artists' voices and showcase their unique perspectives to a global audience.

Key Features

Gallery

Share your digital artwork with a global community of artists and art enthusiasts. Our platform supports various digital art formats and provides a beautiful showcase for your creations.

Community

Connect with fellow artists, follow your favorites, and engage in discussions about art techniques, tools, and trends. Our community is supportive, diverse, and passionate about digital art.

Submission

Share your creative vision with the world through our streamlined submission process. Upload high-quality images, add detailed descriptions, and tag your work to reach the right audience. Participate in challenges and contests to gain exposure.

Homepage

Your personalized gateway to the Picverse experience. Discover trending artworks, featured artists, and curated collections tailored to your interests. Stay updated with the latest community news and events all in one place.
